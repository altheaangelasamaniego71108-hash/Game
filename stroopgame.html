About the Stroop Game

The Stroop Game is based on the Stroop Effect, a psychological test that measures how well a person can focus and manage conflicting information.

How to Play

  1. Click the Start button.
  2. Select your level.
  3. Identify the correct animal.
  4. Earn points when correct.
